Style

Color scheme:

#FFFFFF

White color - to keep the backround neautral,simple and have a standart base for text and visuals to make them more visible and clear

#020924

Dark blue - I chose a darkblue color that could almost be compared to pure black color, just didn’t want it to be too basic. This color might be used for the text shift, in order for the text to be more visible on the light backround.

#D1DAFF

Light purple/gray - I chose this color because it creates a good color match and contrast between all the others.Since grey undertones are usually very neautral and don’t usually affect other color, it can be used as a very good color match for the whole view in general( in this case,the whole website theme)

#FFFB5F

Yellow - thats the color I chose to be the brightest one and bring some dimension to the website.When choosing it, I also picked the coolest undertone to match the other. Since yellow color is usually assosiated with light and happiness , I thought it would be perfect in the whole mixture of the three other cool toned colors.

Typography

The post modernist movement was a large influence for typography. Through rebellion, technology advances and new art styles, new typefaces were created. The ideas from the post modernist movement were non traditional, rebellious, and abstract. This is reflected in the new digital, and abstract typefaces that came out of this period. In the end, through technologies and art innovations, new typefaces were bold, innovative, and for the first time, digital. Thanks to the post modernist movement. Typography that I have chosen for my website is called “ Barlow Condensed” . I chose this style in specific because it’s assoasiated with innovations and modernism.

Text Font

For the text I chose typography with the weight of the style that is not too light and not too heavy,it’s medium and perfect for the website's articles. I also chose this shift because it’s in San Sirif style,which means it’s more modern and not so “old style”.

The numbers are also presented in a very modern structure. What makes the shift so modern is the lenght, which is very vertical and the widght, which is very standart and not so wide, which again,makes it more simplistic,rather than it being very wide,making it look more heavier.
