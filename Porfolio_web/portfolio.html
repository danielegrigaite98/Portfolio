Our group for the same “ Foodsharing Copenhagen” project had to plan the storyboard for the video process making.

It is crucial to make story boards in order for us , or people who are interested in our video to know exactly what to expect in the video and for us to make the planing and filming of the video easier. At first I didn’t know it was even necessary , but after changing the storyboard for a couple of times, we understood that having it will make work easier in the upcoming future.
